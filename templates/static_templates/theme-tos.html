Creative Commons

The material in ufo-host.cise.ufl.edu is a CCBYNCSA collection; all original or remixed content is by default CCBYNCSA. In rare cases, fair-use for noncommercial educational purposes may be employed, but stated as such. If a course includes CCBYND content that content is clearly labeled as CCBYND and the course is clearly labeled as a collection. If you are unsure about the NCSA restriction for specific material, follow the BY attribution to the original source to check.





Difference between Open Access Copyright and CCBYNCSA Licence

Open Access Copyright

Open access copyrighted materials has to be cited or explicitly linked unless for limited fair use.

Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike

CCBYNCSA licensed materials allow users to copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format, as well as remix, transform, and build upon the material, as long as they give attribution.
